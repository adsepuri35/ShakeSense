How it Works

Step by step instructions

Sensor Setup

  • 1. Connect your ShakeSense to your computer through a micro-USB cable.
  • 2. Open the arduino files and upload them to sensor.
  • 3. Make sure the files compiled correctly or the program will not work.
  • 4. Fasten the sensor to your wrist using the velcro strap.
  • 5. Position the sensor so that it faces up while your hand is facing down.

Collect Data From the Sensor

  • 6. Go to the profile page of the website and create an account by entering your phone number and creating a password.
  • 7. Once you have an account, enter your login info and press "log in".
  • 8. Once you're logged in, buttons will appear that will allow you to start collecting data. Press start.
  • 9. After some time, press STOP.
  • 10. A line graph will appear displaying any tremors you may have experienced and their severity.
